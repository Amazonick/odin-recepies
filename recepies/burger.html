Burger

A recepie for the best pizza!

Today, we are going to learn how to make the best burger in the world.

Ingredients:

  • Buns
  • Ground beef
  • 1 cup of cooking oil
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • A pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Grill the meat
  2. Let it rest for 30 seconds
  3. Put tomatoes on the bun
  4. Put cheese on the bun
  5. Eat
