Pizza

A recepie for the best pizza!

Today, we are going to learn how to make the best pizza in the world using only dough, cheese and tomato.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • 1 cup of cooking oil
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Oregano
  • A pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Make dough by mixing 1kg of flower with water and a bit of oil and a pinch of salt
  2. Spread the dough and make it round
  3. Put tomatoes on the dough
  4. Put cheese on the dough
  5. Add oregano
  6. Bake
  7. Eat
